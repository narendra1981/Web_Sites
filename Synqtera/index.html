About SynQtera Technologies

SynQtera Technologies is pioneering the next generation of AI fabric, transforming passive optical interconnects into intelligent, workload-aware co-processors. Our platform enables faster, more efficient AI execution by embedding compute capabilities directly into the fabric.

We partner with innovators to bring real-time, scalable AI acceleration to high-performance environments, delivering seamless integration across data centers and edge networks.

AI Fabric Capabilities

Optical Compute Integration

Embed AI compute directly into optical fabric, turning passive links into active execution paths for high-performance workloads.

Workload-Aware Orchestration
